Commercial
Envelope
Systems

We install, restore, and certify roofing systems engineered for Florida’s most demanding building codes — from HVHZ zones to coastal high-velocity exposure categories.

01

TPO & EPDM Membrane Systems

Single-ply membrane roofing for low-slope commercial and institutional buildings. Mechanically attached, fully adhered, and ballasted assemblies engineered to Florida Building Code specifications.

FBC Compliant NOA Documentation 20-yr Warranty Energy Star
02

Structural Metal Roofing

Standing seam and exposed fastener metal roof systems for industrial, agricultural, institutional, and commercial applications. FM Global-rated assemblies available for wind-critical exposures.

HVHZ Rated FM Global Standing Seam 40-yr Finish
03

Modified Bitumen Systems

SBS and APP-modified bitumen roofing for commercial, multifamily, and mixed-use structures. Torch-applied, cold-applied, and self-adhered configurations engineered for our coastal climate.

SBS / APP Torch & Cold Apply Coastal Grade
04

Built-Up Roofing (BUR)

Multi-ply BUR systems for high-traffic rooftops, mechanical equipment platforms, and demanding commercial applications requiring superior impact resistance and long design life.

Multi-Ply High-Traffic 30+ Year Design Life
05

Restoration & Coating Systems

Silicone, acrylic, and polyurethane roof coatings that extend the serviceable life of existing commercial membrane systems by 10–20 years.

Silicone / Acrylic ENERGY STAR Extend Roof Life

Assessments & Bid Documentation

Pre-bid roof assessments, condition reports, drone documentation, and technical scoping for GCs, property managers, and ownership groups.

How We Work

Bid to
Closeout

A structured process built for commercial clients, GC partners, and bid environments.

1

RFP / Inquiry Submission

Submit your project via our bid inquiry form, direct email, or phone. We review all commercial inquiries within 48 hours.

48-hour response commitment
2

Site Assessment & Scope Review

Field assessment including drone documentation, moisture survey if warranted, and structural review. Existing conditions documented and reconciled against project drawings.

Includes drone survey · Delivered as written report
3

Proposal & System Recommendation

A formal written proposal with system specifications, manufacturer documentation, NOA references, and HVHZ compliance statements where applicable.

Spec-compliant · Manufacturer-backed · NOA Referenced
4

Contract, Permitting & Scheduling

We handle permitting across Florida municipalities — including HVHZ jurisdictions — and coordinate a detailed project schedule that accounts for occupied facilities and GC critical path.

Full permit pull · HVHZ applications · Coordinated scheduling
5

Installation & Quality Control

Installation conducted by trained crews with PE-backed QC inspection protocols. Daily reports, photo documentation, and milestone check-ins standard.

PE QC oversight · Daily documentation · Photo reports
6

Inspection, Warranty & Closeout

Final inspection coordination, manufacturer warranty issuance, permit closeout, as-built documentation, and drone post-installation survey — all standard closeout deliverables.

Warranty registration · Permit closeout · Drone as-built
